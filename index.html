📄 Paper in progress 💻 Open-source framework coming soon

About the Framework

FLSecLAB is a research framework for studying security of collaborative AI systems in distributed environments such as IoT networks, cyber-physical systems, and connected vehicles.

Key Features

  • Simulation of distributed collaborative learning environments
  • Attack and defense modules
  • Control of experimental scenarios
  • Evaluation of robustness and trustworthiness
  • Modular architecture extensible beyond federated learning
